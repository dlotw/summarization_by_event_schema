Nine reservists helping in the U.S. military buildup in the Persian Gulf were among the 13 people killed in the crash of U.S. military cargo plane in West Germany, military authorities said Wednesday .On Monday , two pilots were killed in the crash of an Air Force F-4 Phantom reconnaissance jet in Saudi Arabia , and just hours earlier two Marine Corps UH-1 Huey helicopters , each carrying four crew members , crashed over the Arabian Sea , killing all eight men .A jet trainer crashed Sunday on the flight deck of the aircraft carrier Lexington in the Gulf of Mexico , killing five people , injuring at least two and damaging several aircraft , the Navy said .A top West German military official said Saturday that the fiery crash of a U.S. Air Force jet that killed six people will further erode popular support for national defense programs .The U.S. military aircraft crashed about 800 meters northeast of a Kadena Air Base runway and the crash site is within the air base 's facilities .In addition to those killed in Saudi Arabia , 13 other Air Force personnel were killed in a crash of a C-5 jet cargo plane in Germany .
